Born from a love of Lesotho

We specialize in creating authentic travel experiences that connect visitors with the Kingdom in the Sky. From majestic mountain landscapes and scenic pony trekking routes to cultural village tours and hidden natural wonders — our mission is to showcase the very best of Lesotho.

Based in Botha-Buthe, we've spent over nine years building relationships with local communities, ensuring every tour benefits the people and places you visit.
